## What cookies are
Cookies are small files that a website may store on your device. Similar technologies (e.g., local storage) can be used for related purposes.

## Cookies on this website
This website **does not use first-party cookies** for its basic operation.

### Third-party services (may use cookies)
On the [contact page](/en/contact/), the **“Schedule a call”** button loads a **Cal.com** widget. When you open the booking calendar, Cal.com may set cookies on its own domain (`.cal.com`) to operate the service.

## How to manage cookies
You can configure your browser to:

- Block third-party cookies.
- Delete existing cookies.
- Use private browsing.

Note: blocking third-party cookies may prevent the Cal.com booking widget from working correctly.
